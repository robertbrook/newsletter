Actually, I've not been happier, nor more distressed, ever in my life. Forty has been intense.
 
http://tantek.com/2010/231/b1/bringing-back-the-blog
Tantek and other are still pushing the #indieweb idea forward, but will it be the end of the weblog or the beginning of something new? Aren't those "digital sharecroppers" just too big - and attractive - to be resisted? I mean, why run your own website when you can post to Facebook?
 
http://indiewebcamp.com/2013/UK
It's a way off yet, but save the date. "Rather than posting content on third-party silos of content, we should all own the content we're creating. Publish on your own domain, and syndicate out to silos." IndieWebCampUK 2013 is in Brighton, 8th September. Tantek and Tom Morris and more. 
 
"Unimaginable sunrise."

There's a great quote that Steve Martin said when he was doing standup, 'Comedy is not pretty.'" I saw him perform, ages ago. I'm not sure it was even comedy, but it was remarkable stuff.
