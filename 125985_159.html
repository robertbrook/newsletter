The Personal Names Act of Iceland. "The total number of a person’s forenames, plus his family name, may never be greater than three. ... Forenames shall be capable of having Icelandic genitive endings or shall have become established by tradition in the Icelandic language. Names may not conflict with the linguistic structure of Icelandic. They shall be written in accordance with the ordinary rules of Icelandic orthography unless another orthography is established by tradition. Girls shall be given women’s names and boys shall be given men’s names."
