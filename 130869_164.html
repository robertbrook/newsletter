No, I don't spellcheck or concern myself overmuch with grammar. I'm sorry about that. It's probably as irritating to you as it is to me, but I often write these things in a sort of fugue state, so it's all a bit of a blur. I'm trying to get better.
 
When the #indieweb folk say "Publish on your own domain, and syndicate out to silos" - I get what that means, but it depends on the silos actually picking up what you publish, right? I mean, you can emit an RSS feed and some people will pick that up - but what else is there? You can't syndicate to Twitter, can you? Or to Flickr? Unless syndicate just means double-post. Not that there's anything wrong with that, but it doesn't sound that much like real syndication.Maybe I'm missing something here. I'm also looking at - yet again - using my own local RSS aggregator rather than Google Reader. And what else can be syndicated, other than RSS or Atom?
